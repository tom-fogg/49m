Know Your Number

It's simple and easy to do!

It's calculated using an indication of one month's kWh usage, your usable square meters, and even takes into account the colder winter months and projecting over a 12 month period. Remember that your number is an annual number!

How do I find out my kilowatt-hours?

Grab your electricity bill. This figure is indicated in either units or kWh on the top right of your bill.

How do I know how many square meters of usable space my home has?

Just measure the length and width of each room in your house!

Multiply length by width for each room and then add all the rooms together.

SQM = (width x length) + (width x length)
